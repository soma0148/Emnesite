Our wellbeing is in our hand

We can do the most important thing to do physical exercise regularly. Physical activity improves our brain health, helps to manage weight, reduces the risk of diseases, strengths our bones and muscles, and improves our abilities to do everyday activities. Physical activity has a huge potential to enhance our well-being. Even a short burst of 10 minutes of brisk walking increases our mental alertness, energy and positive mood.
